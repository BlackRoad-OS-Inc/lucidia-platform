Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "request"?

A request is any interaction with Lucidia's AI — a chat message, code execution, memory query, or agent task. Ghost Mode tasks each count as one request regardless of how many sub-tasks the agents perform internally.

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, your current billing period remains active until it expires. Memory is preserved.

What happens to my memory if I downgrade?

Your memory data is always yours. On the Free plan, memory older than 30 days becomes read-only (you can still search it, but new connections are not formed). Upgrading re-activates full memory.

How does the self-hosted option work?

Enterprise customers can deploy Lucidia on their own infrastructure — bare metal, VMs, or Kubernetes. We provide Docker images, Helm charts, and deployment scripts. Your data never leaves your network.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes. Pro comes with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. You will be prompted to add payment only when the trial ends.

What AI models does Lucidia use?

Lucidia runs on BlackRoad's sovereign edge AI infrastructure — 52 TOPS across 5 nodes with local-first inference. We use a mix of open-weight models optimized for code generation, analysis, and reasoning. No data is sent to third-party model providers.

Do you offer annual pricing?

Yes. Annual billing saves 20% — Pro drops to $16/mo and Enterprise to $79/mo. Toggle the billing switch above to see annual prices.

Can I add more team seats to Enterprise?

Enterprise includes 10 seats. Additional seats are $9/mo each. For teams larger than 50, contact us for volume pricing and custom deployment options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for payment processing. All major credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers are accepted. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice with NET-30 terms.

Is my code and data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise customers can self-host for complete control. We never train on your data or share it with third parties. SOC2 Type II certification is available for Enterprise.
